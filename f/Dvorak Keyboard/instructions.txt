# Portable Keyboard Layout: Instructions

- Start in file manager: > Download > Dvorak Keyboard > pkl.exe
- TimeOut (deactivation) after 5 minutes without a keystroke
- Toggle on/off:
	- hit Left Alt + Right Alt at same time
	- in quick access/utility window of taskbar, bottom right of screen, click red or green PKL icon
- Change keybinding and timeout in pkl.ini file
- Requires restart after logging out
